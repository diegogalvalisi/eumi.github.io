Specialized Research

We offer multi- and transdisciplinary specialized research in fields and areas related to European Union–MERCOSUR topics, employing state-of-the-art methodologies and frameworks to understand the bi-regional reality:

  • Research Reports & White Papers
  • Policy Briefs & Position Papers
  • Applied Field Research & Surveys

Our developments are carried out by a multidisciplinary research team, composed of experienced professionals and partner organizations, embedded within a broader network of academia, institutions, and civil society.

Policy Consulting & Strategic Guidance

We provide high-level consulting services to institutions, NGOs, and public actors engaged in EU–Latin America relations. Our advisory services are focused on building bridges between analysis and practice:

  • Policy Development & Strategic Foresight
  • Stakeholder Engagement & Scenario Planning
  • Workshops & Expert Facilitation

Our consulting is informed by rigorous research and tailored to the unique institutional and political contexts of our clients.

Advocacy & Public Engagement

We engage in advocacy initiatives that promote democratic participation, transparency, and cooperation between the EU and Latin America. Our advocacy approach is participatory, rights-based, and evidence-driven:

  • Awareness Campaigns & Policy Dialogue
  • Civil Society Mobilization & Alliances
  • Digital Advocacy & Online Strategies

We work closely with grassroots organizations, media, and institutional partners to shape narratives and influence decision-making processes.

Training & Capacity Building

We design and deliver educational and training programs for professionals, students, and institutional actors seeking to deepen their understanding of EU–MERCOSUR dynamics and policy tools:

  • Workshops, Seminars & Webinars
  • Training-of-Trainers & Leadership Modules
  • Custom Curricula Development

All activities are developed in collaboration with academic institutions, think tanks, and local partners, ensuring relevance and practical impact.
