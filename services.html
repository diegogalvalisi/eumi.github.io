Specialized Research

We offer research in fields and areas related to European Union–MERCOSUR topics, guided by our mission-driven approach and aimed at advancing general knowledge on the subject:

  • Research Reports & White Papers
  • Policy Briefs & Position Papers
  • Applied Field Research & Surveys

Our research is conducted by a multidisciplinary, bi-regional team specialized in EU–MERCOSUR affairs, comprising experienced professionals and partner organizations.

To achieve this, we apply state-of-the-art methodologies—quantitative, qualitative, and mixed methods—supported by up-to-date theoretical frameworks across various fields. This work is continuously enriched by feedback from on-the-ground actors, including decision-makers, academic networks, and civil society at large.

And always having into account the broader network experience and feedback, around the wider academia, institutions, decision-makers, and community organizations, ensuring relevance and impact.

Policy Consulting & Strategic Guidance

We offer close head-by-head, collaborative consulting services to public institutions, civil society organizations, and businesses seeking to strengthen or initiate actions related to EU–MERCOSUR relations.

  • Policy Development & Strategic Foresight
  • Stakeholder Engagement & Scenario Planning
  • Workshops & Expert Facilitation

Our aim is to support decision-making and strategic planning by turning in-depth analysis into actionable, impactful strategies. Our consulting is rooted in our accumulated evidence-based research, our own life experience in the regions, and a strong ethical foundation.

In this spirit, we partner with initiatives that align with our mission, values, and shared commitment to enhancing the EU–MERCOSUR partnership.

Advocacy & Public Engagement

We lead advocacy initiatives that promote the strengthening and deepening of the EU–MERCOSUR partnership across multiple fronts.

  • Awareness Campaigns & Policy Dialogue
  • Network Enhancement
  • Ethical Lobbying

Our advocacy efforts are driven by a strong commitment to fostering a more resilient, inclusive, and effective bi-regional partnership. We engage directly with real-life contexts and key actors to ensure our actions reflect on-the-ground realities.

The Institute approach is open, transparent, ethically oriented, and participatory. We collaborate closely with grassroots organizations, media, businesses, and institutional partners to shape public narratives and influence decision-making processes.

Training & Capacity Building

We are committed to sharing our knowledge, tools and general experience to other people and organizations that seek to improve their understanding and action towards the enhancement of the general EU-Mercosur relations and the topics that it concerns. By the provision of different services:

  • Workshops, Seminars & Webinars
  • Training-of-Trainers & Leadership Modules
  • Custom Curricula Development

Our programs are tailored to meet the diverse needs of learners and practitioners working at the intersection of diplomacy, development, trade, governance, and civil society engagement.

Every engagement is customized to the specific institutional, social, and geopolitical realities of our partners, ensuring that strategies are not only sound in theory but also feasible and impactful in practice.
